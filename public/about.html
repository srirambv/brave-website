About Brave

  • Team
  • Overview

We have a mission to save the web by increasing browsing speed and safety for users, while funding content creators.

The web has become a different place. Advertising has become a weapon rather than just a revenue stream for publishers. With the ad ecosystem out of control, users have revolted and blocking ads has become the new weapoin of choice. But this results in a race to the bottom and at this bottom, nobody wins. Without the ability for content creators to earn money for their efforts, thei will need to close down. Users will be left with nowhere to browse, relegated to hand-picked content from slanted sources.

Brave is saving the web by building a new suite of leading-edge web browsers, connected securly, and offering new solutions to help keep publishers in business.
