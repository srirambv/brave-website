Brave

Brave Youth Program acts as a stepping stone for college students to get introduced to the open source community, and to gain practical knowledge from a wide array of dynamic, successful, and experienced people.

Goals & Mission

Leadership

By stepping up to the challenge of building and growing a community with a vision, Brave Youths learn the most fundamental aspects of leadership first-hand.

Coaching

Coaching and leadership programs make valuable, practical knowledge more available to communities by fostering interactions between experienced and proactive people.

Participation

Brave Youths run squads at their universities to build a Brave community and to support the cause. Brave Youth Program will be a one huge stepping stone to keep the web safer.
